Frequently Asked Questions

The initial franchise fee is $27,500, and the estimated initial investment ranges from $99,500 to $155,000. Please refer to items 5, 6 and 7 (pages 4-11) of the Uniform Franchise Offering Circular for a complete list of costs associated with our franchise.

*The Uniform Franchise Offering Circular will be sent upon receipt of your completed franchise application or upon request at toll free # 877-757-1445.

The estimated initial investment covers the initial franchise fee, the turn-key kiosk fee and training fees. Please refer to item 7 of the Uniform Franchise Offering Circular for a complete list of costs associated with our franchise.

We believe it is feasible for a franchisee to successfully operate more than one franchise. Proposals for multiple locations will be subject to franchisor evaluation and will be approved on a case by case basis.

Operational training currently takes place at our corporate headquarters in Waco, Texas. Our three-day training program will equip you with the right tools to launch and operate your franchise.

After completing the qualification process, we will refer you to a realtor that will assist you in finding and securing a location in a retail mall. Showcolate will provide you with site criteria specifications to help you make your decision on the best location for your franchise, and will review your location choice for final approval.
