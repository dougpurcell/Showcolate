About Showcolate

History

Showcolate began in Belo Horizonte, Brazil, from where it launched into new markets throughout the country, becoming one of the most successful Brazilian franchises in recent years. Since then, Showcolate has expanded internationally by opening corporate headquarters in the US and Portugal.

Showcolate franchises operate from our state-of-the-art kiosks, which feature our famous chocolate-spinning wheels! This main feature displays a cascading flow of chocolate rotating on five spinning wheels behind a glass window. Visible dark, milk and white chocolates circulate as they are prepared for our sweet fondue. Our innovative kiosk system is made to ensure maximum profitability at prime locations.

Designed for retail malls, the sweet success of Showcolate has been tasted in more than 100 locations worldwide that sell over 20 delicious products. And we truly believe this is just the beginning! Showcolate has teamed up with the Findley Group, a franchise consulting firm with over 20 years of franchising experience specializing in innovative business concepts. Together we have developed a franchise system that makes this Brazilian concept perfect for the U.S. market.
